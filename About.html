Homepage The Problem Take Action!

One fateful day, I was in class and overheard from my classmate how they were on a diet, their reason being is that they want to get skinnier. My classmate thinks they look fat, they said that, and I was baffled! They seemed to be in average shape. I realized how dangerous this was, especially since that classmate’s diet plan is to not eat anything, just so they can lose weight.

I feared this may cause more harm than good, you shouldn’t harm or starve yourself, and you shouldn’t want to lose weight because of wanting to get that “perfect” body. After that day, I wanted to talk about this to my friends because that event got me really upset. Me and my friends started talking about their experiences about poor eating habits, hearing all these stories make me sick. I wish I could’ve done something to not let anyone suffer through those types of problems, then I realize that wish is possible! So I then grouped up my friends and talked about wanting to become a group to advocate about poor eating habits and surprisingly they all agreed.

This is me, the head of the group! I am Gabrielle Mari V. Siangco, you may call me Mari, I am 14 years old and I study in Philippine Science High School Southern Mindanao Campus. I dream of wanting to help others overcome their problems, which is one of the reasons why I am forming this advocacy.
